Theme:
Dracula's Castle/Dungeon - Inspired by the supermario bros level

Rooms:
1 room with 2 doors. 1 big door and 1 small. Small is for levels and big is for ending.
Small door goes to different room each time, so new level. First room is last room.
Boss fight spawns after all minor enemies are defeated. If player wins he can open the 
big door and win.
